Privacy Policy

Last Updated: February 4, 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how your personal information is collected, used, and shared when you use our LinkedIn integration application ("the App").

Information We Collect

When you authenticate with LinkedIn through our App, we may collect the following information:

  • Profile Information: Your name, profile picture, and LinkedIn profile URL
  • Email Address: Your primary email address associated with your LinkedIn account
  • LinkedIn User ID: A unique identifier for your LinkedIn account

How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Authenticate you and provide access to the App's features
  • Post content to LinkedIn on your behalf (only when you explicitly request it)
  • Display your profile information within the App

Data Storage

Your LinkedIn access tokens are stored locally on your device. We do not store your personal information on external servers. All data processing happens locally.

Data Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or otherwise transfer your personal information to third parties. Your data is only used to facilitate the connection between you and LinkedIn's services.

Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access the personal data we hold about you
  • Request deletion of your data
  • Revoke access to your LinkedIn account at any time through LinkedIn's settings

LinkedIn's Privacy Policy

Our App uses LinkedIn's API services. Please also review LinkedIn's Privacy Policy to understand how LinkedIn handles your data.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the "Last Updated" date.
