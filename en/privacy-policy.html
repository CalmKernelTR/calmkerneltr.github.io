Privacy Policy

1. Data Controller

The data controller for this website is CalmKernel. For any questions or requests regarding your personal data, you can reach us at meet@calmkernel.tr.

2. Data Collected

This website only uses technical cookies set by Cloudflare for security purposes. No Google Analytics or third-party tracking cookies are used.

All fonts used on this site are self-hosted. No external font services such as Google Fonts CDN are used.

3. Processing of Personal Data

Email addresses and message content submitted through the contact form are used solely for communication purposes. This data is not shared with third parties and is deleted once the communication process is completed.

4. Cookies

This site only uses Cloudflare security cookies (__cf_bm). These cookies are session-based and are automatically deleted when you close your browser.

5. Your Rights

Under KVKK (Turkish Data Protection Law) Article 11 and the GDPR, you have the following rights:

  • Right to know whether your personal data is being processed
  • Right to request information about the processing
  • Right to request correction of your personal data
  • Right to request deletion or destruction of your personal data
  • Right to object to the processing

You can submit your requests to meet@calmkernel.tr.

6. Contact

For any questions about our privacy policy, please contact us at meet@calmkernel.tr.
