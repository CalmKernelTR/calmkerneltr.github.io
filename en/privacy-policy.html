Last Updated: February 17, 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how your personal information is collected, processed, and protected when you visit the calmkernel.tr website ("Site"). It has been prepared in accordance with the Turkish Personal Data Protection Law No. 6698 ("KVKK") and the EU General Data Protection Regulation ("GDPR").

1. Data Controller

The data controller for your personal data:

  • Project: CalmKernel
  • Website: calmkernel.tr
  • Email: meet@calmkernel.tr

2. Data We Collect

The Site adheres to the principle of collecting the minimum amount of data possible:

  • Technical data: IP address, browser type, and operating system information (automatically processed by Cloudflare CDN infrastructure for security and performance purposes)
  • Local storage (localStorage): Your theme preference (light/dark mode) is stored only in your browser and is not transmitted to our servers

The Site does not use any analytics tools (Google Analytics, Facebook Pixel, etc.). Font files are self-hosted and no requests are sent to third-party servers.

3. Cookies and Similar Technologies

The Site does not directly use cookies. However, our CDN provider Cloudflare may place technical cookies (__cf_bm) for bot protection purposes. These cookies are solely for security and do not create personal profiles.

Your theme preference is stored in your browser's localStorage feature. localStorage is not a cookie and is not transmitted to servers.

4. Purposes of Data Processing

Technical data collected is processed only for the following purposes:

  • Ensuring website security (DDoS protection, bot prevention)
  • Ensuring proper technical operation of the website
  • Remembering user preferences (theme selection)

5. Data Sharing

Your personal data is not sold, rented, or shared for commercial purposes with third parties.

As part of the technical infrastructure, data may be shared with the following service providers:

  • Cloudflare, Inc. (USA) — CDN and security services. Cloudflare Privacy Policy
  • GitHub, Inc. (USA) — Website hosting (GitHub Pages). GitHub Privacy Statement

6. LinkedIn Integration

When you use LinkedIn integration tools developed by CalmKernel, additional data related to your LinkedIn account (profile information, email address, LinkedIn user ID) may be processed. This data is stored locally on your device only and is not sent to external servers. For LinkedIn's own privacy policy: LinkedIn Privacy Policy

7. Your Rights

Under KVKK Article 11 and GDPR Articles 15-22, you have the following rights:

  • Learn whether your personal data is being processed
  • Request information about the processing of your personal data
  • Learn the purpose of processing and whether your data is being used in accordance with its purpose
  • Know the third parties to whom your personal data is transferred
  • Request correction of incomplete or inaccurate personal data
  • Request deletion or destruction of your personal data
  • Object to any adverse result arising from the analysis of your personal data exclusively through automated systems
  • Claim compensation for damages resulting from unlawful processing of your personal data

To exercise your rights, please contact meet@calmkernel.tr. Requests will be resolved within 30 days at the latest.

8. Data Security

To ensure the security of your personal data, HTTPS encryption, Cloudflare WAF firewall, and regular security updates are applied.

9. Changes to This Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated from time to time. Changes will be published on this page and the "Last Updated" date will be revised.
